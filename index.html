Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredienti

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not neccesary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    2.

in a medium.size mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the doug, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
